# Satnam Intelligence Commercial Model

## Objective

Create recurring revenue from high-integrity digital-asset intelligence while preserving a meaningful free public-service layer.

Revenue is earned from research access, data delivery, archives, exports, and API usage—not from custody, trading, referral pressure, promised returns, or community-fund management.

## Product ladder

### 1. Community Brief — Free

- daily executive summary
- selected BTC, LTC, and XLM developments
- selected ETF and treasury changes
- one policy update
- one Morpho risk observation
- public source links
- correction notices

Purpose: establish trust, serve the community, and create an auditable public record.

### 2. Satnam Intelligence Pro — Launch target: $21/month or $210/year

- complete daily institutional report
- ETF flow tables
- treasury and sovereign leaderboards
- BTC, LTC, and XLM network sections
- CLARITY Act and policy tracker
- Morpho cbBTC/cbLTC dashboard
- PDF and web editions
- historical comparisons
- confidence and methodology notes

The launch price is a hypothesis and may change after customer interviews, cost analysis, and legal/tax review.

### 3. Institutional — Launch target: $210/month or $2,100/year

- all Pro content
- JSON and CSV exports
- report archive
- API allowance
- factual organization and asset watchlists
- team access
- redistribution and white-label rights only where contractually granted

No bespoke portfolio recommendations are included.

### 4. Agent API — x402 sandbox first

Proposed pay-per-request endpoints:

- latest treasury changes: $0.01
- legislation/status lookup: $0.01
- Morpho market snapshot: $0.05
- enriched cross-source entity profile: $0.10
- latest full report JSON: $1.00

Production pricing will be based on source costs, compute, settlement costs, support burden, and customer value.

### 5. One-time research products

- sector or country report
- public-company treasury forensic report
- Litecoin institutional adoption report
- Stellar tokenization/RWA report
- Morpho market-risk report

One-time reports must use a written scope, evidence standard, delivery terms, and conflict disclosures.

## Revenue allocation policy

All revenue should enter a dedicated operating account or wallet that is separate from:

- charitable/langar funds
- public donation addresses
- long-term BTC/LTC treasury
- customer funds
- future agent experimental budgets

Illustrative operating allocation, subject to accounting and governance review:

- 50% data, infrastructure, engineering, and operations
- 20% reserve and security budget
- 15% community/free-report subsidy
- 10% sales, partnerships, and contributor rewards
- 5% contingency and corrections reserve

No automatic conversion of operating revenue into leveraged positions is permitted in Phase 1.

## Distribution channels

- Satnam.x web publication
- daily email
- downloadable PDF
- JSON/CSV feeds
- agent-to-agent x402 endpoints
- selected public posts on social channels
- licensed white-label editions

## Customer segments

- BTC, LTC, and XLM holders seeking organized factual intelligence
- digital-asset treasury companies
- analysts and research desks
- family offices and professional investors
- protocol, wallet, and infrastructure teams
- journalists and educators
- AI agents purchasing structured data

## Editorial independence

- sponsorships cannot change scores or suppress corrections
- paid issuer profiles must be clearly labeled
- affiliate relationships must be disclosed
- holdings and conflicts of interest must be disclosed under a written policy
- advertisements must remain separate from research conclusions

## Legal and compliance design target

The publication is designed as general, impersonal, bona fide research of regular circulation. It must not become individualized portfolio advice through subscriber chats, custom alerts, or agent responses.

Before paid launch, obtain qualified review of:

- investment-adviser and publisher-exclusion analysis
- terms of use and research disclaimer
- privacy and data-retention policy
- source licenses and permitted redistribution
- subscription, cancellation, and refund terms
- sales-tax and income-accounting treatment
- sanctions and payment-screening obligations where applicable
- marketing, testimonials, and performance-presentation rules

## Launch metrics

- free subscribers
- paid conversion rate
- monthly recurring revenue
- churn
- report open and completion rates
- API calls and revenue per endpoint
- source coverage and freshness
- correction rate
- percentage of headline claims with A-grade evidence
- infrastructure cost per report

## Non-negotiable rules

- no guaranteed profit, yield, or loss protection
- no fabricated or uncited numbers
- no hidden sponsored conclusions
- no custody of subscriber funds
- no trade execution in the research product
- no sale or redistribution of third-party data beyond permitted rights
- no commingling of revenue, donations, treasury, or customer assets
